Most teams don't fail from lack of talent. They fail from learning too slowly.

This really hits home when you see some of the most talented people with failed projects or companies.

This is the core problem Corvo Labs exists to solve. We help teams move from uncertainty to clarity, and get there fast.

---

## Why "Corvo"?

Crows are awesome. Crows are corvids. Corvo is Portuguese and Italian for corvid. But really, it's all about the crows.

Crows and ravens are remarkable creatures. They've been observed solving complex, multi-step puzzles faster than human children<sup>[1]</sup>. They build and modify tools—improving their designs across generations<sup>[2]</sup>. They remember human faces for years and share knowledge about threats with other crows across vast distances and generations<sup>[3]</sup>.

Other birds use tools too. The woodpecker finch pries insects from bark with twigs<sup>[4]</sup>. Egyptian vultures crack open eggs with stones<sup>[5]</sup>. Green herons drop bait to lure fish<sup>[6]</sup>. But corvids stand apart for their problem-solving sophistication and their ingenuity—extending their capabilities through tools.

This is one of the key ideas behind Corvo Labs: using tools to extend what humans can do. AI has been a powerful leverage point for human creativity and exceptionalism. We use AI to supplement and extend human capability, not replace it.

## What Corvo Labs Does

We're a product consultancy with a specialty in AI-enablement and healthcare technology. Our focus is on fixed-term projects with success clearly defined at the start. We bring focus and direction to uncertainty, helping you de-risk your next move.

Our clients fall into two main categories:

**Technology startups** — pre-product-market fit, or in the middle of refining product-market fit. We help you define, prioritize, and test hypotheses so you can move forward with confidence and significantly reduced risk.

**Healthcare delivery organizations** — looking to understand their business, standardize operations, and identify efficiency opportunities in anticipation of scaling. We map your workflows, build the documentation your team needs, and help you see where automation and AI can make the biggest impact.

Both types of clients share the same need: clarity under uncertainty.
